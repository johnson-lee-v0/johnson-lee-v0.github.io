ABOUT

SUMMARY

Using a similar approach to the NBA Win Loss Predictor project data was pulled off of basketballreference.com for the Boston Celtics from the 2017-2022 season to understand the development of Jayson Tatum and Jaylen Brown

INSTALLATION & USAGE

Github Repo

INSPIRATION

In the NBA 2022 Finals Boston lost to the Warriors in 6 games. When looking at the Celtics most of the attention goes to Jayson Tatum and Jaylen Brown given the storyline painted of how they have played together since Jayson Tatum's rookie season. I wanted to take a look through pass season see how they progressed and if they impact the game as much as the ESPN analyst tries to sell it

TECHNOLOGIES

Python was used to conduct exploratory analysis and build out Naives Bayes Model, KNN and SVM model

FUTURE IDEAS AND WHATS NEXT

Further analysis the data and see if it possible to create model that can predict the playoffs as the data for regular season and the playoff look very different given how players play
