SUMMARY

As part of the Predictive Analytics course (AFM 346), I completed a series of projects that utilized popular datasets such as Gapminder, Default, Wine Scoring, NASDAQ Index Over Time, and more. Each project aimed to investigate a business problem found within the dataset, and I applied the CRISP DM model to guide my analysis. In the modelling section, I used various machine learning models to come up with solutions and next steps actions fo the business problem
