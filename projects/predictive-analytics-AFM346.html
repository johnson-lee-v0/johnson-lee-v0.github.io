ABOUT

SUMMARY

A compilation of projects done in the Predictive Analytics course (AFM 346). The projects uses popular dataset such as Gapminder, Default, Wine Scoring, NASDAQ Index Over Time and more. Each project aimed to investitgate a business problem found within the dataset. The CRISP DM model was applied and differnt machine learning model was used for the modelling section to come up with solutions and next steps.
