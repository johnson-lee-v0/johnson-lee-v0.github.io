ABOUT

SUMMARY

This project aimed to look at the Zillow Rent Index for the New York MSA area to gain a better understanding of the market

INSTALLATION & USAGE

Github Repo

INSPIRATION

The idea to pull data from Zillow to learn more about rent prices was from the thought of wanting to move to New York in the future. I wanted to know what kind of scenario would cause rent to lower and make it more affordable

TECHNOLOGIES

Data was extracted through Quandl & Zillow Research for the housing and rent portion while FRED was used to get local,state and country wide indicators. Python was then used to build ML models to make prediction

FUTURE IDEAS AND WHATS NEXT

Through this exploration, I want to look into a certain zip-code deeper and understand the dynamics of the local housing market and figure out if buying or renting would be better
